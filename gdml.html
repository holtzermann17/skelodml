The discussion was largely led by Stephen Wolfram with Michael Kohlhase recording matters on the whiteboard. It ranged over material familiar in some respects to all of us, but the discussions helped the group realize better their common purpose. Obviously only part of the context could be broached, and we already have the much larger NRC report to work with.

Rough notes derived from the board are as follows, organized as a number of Goal points, as follows:

  • [G1]

    Material Acquisition - Digitize All Mathematics. The general idea is to digitize all mathematical documents so as to have the legacy of the mathematical corpus available. Although clearly unrealizable, it is a good simple goal.

  • [G2]

    IDs and Metadata ∀\forall Math. Indexing must be provided and databases produced about the documents of mathematics that are assembled.

  • [G3]

    Add Presentation Markup ∀\forall Math (pMathML / LaTeX). In particular, TeX\TeX, character PDF, and markups that specify the documents in a way that is more computationally accessible should be provided.

  • [G4]

    Create a Target Format for Semantic Math. An intermediate-level semantic representation language for mathematical literature, between presentation and full formalization, and applicable to enhance the availability if the knowledge in the mathematical corpus needs developing.

  • [G5]

    Advanced Services and Use Cases beyond [G2-G4].

    • –

      ”Wolfram Alpha” for all math literature

    • –

      Conflict propagation over the literature (we need to know what is true)

    • –

      Topic modeling for mathematics

    • –

      Support for such queries as ”What papers say something about my paper?”

    • –

      Finding results that are implicit in a document set

    • –

      Finding theorems applicable in a context

    • –

      Illuminating tenure and promotion cases

    • –

      Facilitating search for plagiarism and reinvention

    • –

      Mathematical recommender systems

  • [G6]

    WDML Portal(s)/App(s)/API(s). A GDML will offer new services through Apps and must offer public APIs to its data.

  • [G7]

    Models of Sustainability and Extensions. The organizational model of a GDML for the ages has to be set up.

  • [G8]

    Subcontract Processing for Funds / Hiring for WDML. In the initial stages there has to be an organizational center that can receive funds, enter into contracts and hire workers.

  • [G9]

    Mathematical linguistics. The language of mathematics needs more careful analysis extending natural language processing.
